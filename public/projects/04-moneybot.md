# moneybot
_giving an AI financial assistant a face_

moneybot is cash app's AI-powered personal financial assistant — it helps users manage, track, and optimize their money. it needed a character

### the brief

the product team had the functionality. what they didn't have was a personality. moneybot needed to feel approachable without being childish, smart without being cold. it had to work at 16px in a chat bubble and at full bleed in marketing
